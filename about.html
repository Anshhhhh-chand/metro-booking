Welcome to MetroGo

MetroGo is a revolutionary metro booking system developed by students of ABV-IIITM Gwalior. Our mission is to provide a seamless and user-friendly platform for metro ticket booking, making public transportation more accessible and convenient for everyone.

About ABV-IIITM Gwalior

Atal Bihari Vajpayee Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management Gwalior (ABV-IIITM) is a premier institution established in 1997. It's one of the first IIITs in India and has consistently maintained its position as a center of excellence in technical education.

Our Mission

We aim to transform the way people book metro tickets by providing a digital platform that is efficient, secure, and easy to use. Our system helps reduce waiting times, eliminate the need for physical tickets, and contribute to a more sustainable environment.

Our Team

This project is developed by a team of passionate students from ABV-IIITM Gwalior, who are committed to using technology to solve real-world problems. Our team combines technical expertise with innovative thinking to create solutions that make a difference.
